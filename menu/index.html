Our Menu

Traditional Mexican flavors, made fresh daily.

Tacos

Al Pastor, Carnitas, Barbacoa, Birria, Carne Asada, Pollo, Chorizo, and Lengua. Each served on double corn tortillas with onion, cilantro, and your choice of salsa.

Burritos & Bowls

Oversized flour tortillas packed with your choice of protein, rice, beans, cheese, sour cream, and fresh toppings. Available as a bowl over rice for a lighter option.

Quesadillas & More

Handmade quesadillas with melted Oaxacan cheese and your choice of protein. Plus tamales, elote, churros, and agua frescas made fresh daily.

Sides & Drinks

Mexican rice, refried beans, chips and guacamole, and our famous horchata, jamaica, and tamarindo agua frescas.
