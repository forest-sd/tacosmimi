Our Story

From Mimi's kitchen to your table.

A Family Tradition

Tacos Mimi started with a grandmother's recipes and a passion for sharing authentic Mexican food with the community. Mimi spent her life perfecting the art of the taco — from the masa for the tortillas to the precise blend of dried chilies in her signature salsas. Every dish on our menu carries her legacy of love, generosity, and unwavering commitment to doing things the right way, even when the easy way would be faster.

We believe that great food brings people together. Our restaurant is a gathering place where families, friends, and strangers share tables, stories, and the universal language of delicious food. Whether you're a taco purist or trying Mexican street food for the first time, you're welcome at Mimi's table.
