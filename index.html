Welcome to Tacos Mimi

Tacos Mimi is a vibrant eatery dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Mexican street tacos to your table. Every taco is handmade with fresh tortillas, premium meats slow-cooked to perfection, and topped with traditional salsas and garnishes that honor the recipes of Mexico's taqueros. From classic al pastor and carnitas to barbacoa and birria, each bite is a journey to the streets of Mexico City, Guadalajara, and Oaxaca.

Fresh Tortillas
